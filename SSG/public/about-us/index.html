About Our Business

At Ottawa Elite Construction, our more than 20 years in the general contracting business is a journey marked by dedication, expertise, and a deep-seated commitment to our clients. Originating from Winnipeg, MB, and now serving Ottawa, Ontario and surrounding areas, our family-operated firm has become a symbol of trust and quality in both residential and commercial construction. Fully licensed and insured, we take pride in our BBB accreditation, a reflection of our ethical and customer-focused approach.
Our services are comprehensive, ranging from building new homes, Second Dwelling Units (SDU's), and handling commercial projects, to house flips, renovations, and restoration work. We collaborate with trusted trade partners in electrical, HVAC, septic, plumbing, and masonry to ensure every aspect of your project is managed with utmost professionalism. Our goal is to provide you with on-budget solutions and to respect your timelines, guaranteeing satisfaction without compromise.
